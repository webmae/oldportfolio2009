Private Individuals

We collect and sort out in our yard all sort of scrap metal. Private individuals can either bring old metals directly to our yard in Lot or we can also provide containers for larger quantities that have to be loaded directly

We accept all kind of ferrous and non ferrous scrap metal: aluminium, zinc, iron, lead, brass etc. Metals should not have any plastic, glass or wooden attachments.

For safety reasons we do not accept closed safes, gas cylinders, tyres, radioactive elements or uncut tanks.

Metals are sorted out, weighted and, according to the type of material and rate of the day, are paid in cash, cheque or by bank transfer.

For larger quantities we propose free container service.

According to the law in force we demand you to bring an identity card.

Business

Our aim is to establish long-term, based on mutual trust, partnerships with our present and future clients and suppliers

Companies can bring all kind of metal scrap to our yard in Lot.

If the company doesn’t have a possibility to bring their scrap metal to our yard we can provide our containers in which the metal will be loaded directly at the company’s address.

Payments are done by cheque or bank transfer.

Infrastructure

Equipped with two articulated lorry and three dumper trucks, AG Metals Recycling assures collecting of metal out-take : collect in factories, on demolition sites, sorting out centres or waste reception centres.

For larger quantities we provide containers from 10 m3 to 35 m3.
