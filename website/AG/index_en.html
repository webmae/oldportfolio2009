AG Metals Recycling is a family-run company located in the South of Brussels and spread on an area of one hectare and specialised in ferrous and non ferrous metals recycling. We collect and recycle all types of metal scrap coming from industry and private business: iron, aluminium, brass, milberry, birch cliff, zinc, lead etc.

AG Metals Recycling contributes to the environment protection by transforming metals into a renewable resource. Our objective is ferrous (iron) and non ferrous (aluminium, zinc, copper, brass etc) metal waste recovery and recycling. Metals are sorted by categories before being forwarded to different foundries. Thus we offer raw material for various industries and contribute to the pollution diminution and energy and natural resources saving.
